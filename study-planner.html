What is an AI Study Planner?

An AI Study Planner is a smart educational tool that helps students create optimized study schedules based on available time, subjects, and academic goals. Instead of manually planning study sessions, students can generate a structured timetable instantly.

Effective planning improves productivity, reduces stress, and helps students prepare more efficiently for exams, quizzes, and assignments.

Benefits of Using an AI Study Planner

  • Create balanced study schedules.
  • Improve time management skills.
  • Reduce exam stress.
  • Increase academic productivity.
  • Focus on high-priority subjects.
  • Develop consistent study habits.

How Smart Study Planning Works

The planner analyzes your subjects, available daily study hours, and remaining days before exams. It then distributes study time equally across subjects, helping you stay organized and productive.
