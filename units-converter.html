What Is a Unit Converter?

A Unit Converter is a tool that converts measurements from one unit to another. It is widely used in education, engineering, science, business, and everyday calculations.

Length Conversion Scale

  • 1 Kilometer = 1000 Meters
  • 1 Meter = 100 Centimeters

Weight Conversion Scale
