What is a Percentage Calculator?

A Percentage Calculator is a simple tool that helps students calculate their percentage scores quickly and accurately. It is commonly used for exam results, assignments, quizzes, and academic performance tracking.

How to Calculate Percentage?

The percentage formula is:

Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

For example, if you score 450 marks out of 550:

(450 ÷ 550) × 100 = 81.82%
