What is a GPA Scale Converter?

A GPA Scale Converter helps students convert grades between different academic grading systems. Universities around the world use various GPA scales, including 4.0, 5.0 and 10.0 systems.

When applying for admissions, scholarships, internships or international study programs, students often need to convert their GPA into a different scale.

How GPA Conversion Works

The converter first converts your GPA into a percentage of the original scale and then calculates the equivalent GPA on the target scale.

For example:

  • 3.5 / 4.0 = 87.5%
  • Equivalent on 10.0 scale = 8.75
  • Equivalent on 5.0 scale = 4.38

What GPA scale do most universities use?

Many universities use the 4.0 GPA scale, although some countries use 5.0 or 10.0 systems.

Can GPA conversion affect admissions?

Yes. Universities often evaluate applications using their own grading scale.
